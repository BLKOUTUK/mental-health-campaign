## Icon Specifications

### Primary Icons (Bold, Simple, Recognizable)

**Building Icon** (Community Centres):
- Simplified architectural silhouette
- Warm Gold (#F4A261) fill
- White outline
- Represents: Physical community spaces

**House Icon** (Affordable Housing):
- Simple house shape with roof
- Warm Gold fill
- Heart symbol in center (community care)
- Represents: Land trust housing

**Gavel Icon** (Legal Advocacy):
- Judge's gavel silhouette
- Warm Gold fill
- Represents: Justice, legal protection

**Camera/Film Icon** (Community Media):
- Movie camera or film reel
- Warm Gold fill
- Represents: Community-owned documentation

**Hands Joining Icon** (Peer Support):
- Two hands clasping/supporting
- Warm Gold fill
- Represents: Mutual aid, community care

**Heart with Pulse Line** (Crisis Care):
- Heart shape with EKG line
- Warm Gold fill
- Represents: Mental health support, life-saving care

**Celebration Icon** (Joy):
- Raised hands in celebration OR confetti burst
- Warm Gold fill
- Represents: Joy as healing, not just trauma treatment

**Single Person Silhouette** (Per Person Cost):
- Simple human figure
- White fill
- Used to show "per person" framing

**Radiator Icon** (Winter Fuel Payment):
- Simple radiator/heater shape
- Used in comparison graphic
- Represents: What pensioners get (£500)

## Visual Infographic Elements

### Bar Graph (Cost Comparison):
- Horizontal bars showing relative costs
- This programme (£543/year): Small bar, Warm Gold
- Psychiatric admission (£10K/year): Medium bar, white outline
- Homelessness costs (£73K/year): Large bar, white outline
- Clean, minimal design - numbers speak for themselves

### Population Grid (35,000 People):
- 350 small person icons in 35 rows × 10 columns grid
- Warm Gold icons on red background
- Visual representation of community size
- Shows this is about real people, not abstract numbers

### Timeline Circles (5 Years):
- 5 circles representing years
- Progressively filled to show investment over time
- Warm Gold circles
- Simple, clear progression visual

### ROI Coin Visual:
- Single £1 coin on left (white/gold)
- Arrow pointing right
- Pile of 8-12 coins on right
- Clean, immediate visual of return on investment

## Typography Hierarchy

**Hero Number (£543)**:
- Poppins ExtraBold, 120pt, white
- Largest element on poster
- Immediate visual impact

**"PER PERSON PER YEAR"**:
- Poppins Bold, 36pt, white
- Directly below hero number
- Makes cost personal and annual

**Section Headers** ("WHAT YOU GET", etc.):
- Poppins Bold, 32pt, white, all caps
- Clear section divisions

**Icon Labels**:
- Inter SemiBold, 20pt, white
- Brief, punchy descriptions next to icons
- Maximum 5-7 words each

**Comparison Text** ("Less than winter fuel payment"):
- Inter Medium, 24pt, white
- Key comparison framing

**Body Text** (minimal - "Making Equality Real" section):
- Inter Regular, 18pt, white
- Maximum 3 short sentences
- Only where absolutely necessary

**£95 MILLION**:
- Poppins ExtraBold, 72pt, white
- Secondary hero number

**Footer Information**:
- Inter Regular, 14pt, white
- Minimal, essential contact/attribution only

## Color Application Strategy

**Background**:
- BLKOUT Red (#D4261A) full bleed
- Bold, revolutionary, impossible to ignore

**Primary Text**:
- White (#FFFFFF) for maximum contrast
- High readability on red background

**Icons & Accents**:
- Warm Gold (#F4A261) for all icons
- Creates visual consistency
- Warm, welcoming, community-focused

**Divider Lines**:
- White or Warm Gold (3pt thickness)
- Clean section separation
- Double lines for major sections, single for subsections

**Infographic Elements**:
- Warm Gold for "our programme" (positive, affordable)
- White outline for comparison costs (stark, expensive)
- Visual distinction between affordable solution and costly status quo

## Design Principles for This Poster

### Visual Hierarchy
1. **£543** - Your eye goes here first (hero number)
2. **Icons with brief labels** - Quick scan shows what you get
3. **Bar graph comparison** - Visual proof this is cheaper
4. **Population grid** - Human scale of who benefits
5. **£95M / 5 years** - Total investment context
6. **ROI visual** - Financial case in one image
7. **Footer** - Where to learn more

### Information Density
- **80% visual, 20% text**
- Icons replace paragraphs
- Infographics replace explanations
- Numbers tell the story
- Brief labels only where necessary

### Accessibility
- High contrast (white on red, gold on red)
- Large, bold typography
- Clear icon shapes (recognizable at distance)
- Simple infographics (immediate comprehension)
- Alternative text for all visual elements (digital version)

### Cultural Resonance
- **Red = Revolutionary energy** (BLKOUT brand, urgency, power)
- **Gold = Community wealth** (warmth, richness, solidarity)
- **Icons = Universal language** (crosses literacy barriers)
- **Infographics = Democratic access** (visual data for all)

## Production Specifications

### Print Version (Street Posters)
- **Size**: A2 (420mm × 594mm)
- **Paper**: Heavyweight poster paper (200gsm) or vinyl for weather resistance
- **Finish**: Matte or gloss (matte for outdoor readability)
- **Color Mode**: CMYK
  - Red #D4261A = C:0 M:90 Y:86 K:17
  - Gold #F4A261 = C:0 M:32 Y:60 K:4
  - White = C:0 M:0 Y:0 K:0
- **Bleed**: 3mm all sides
- **Safe Area**: 10mm margin from trim

### Digital Version (Social Media)
- **Full Poster**: 1754px × 2480px (A2 proportions, 300dpi)
- **Instagram Post**: 1080px × 1350px (4:5 crop, hero number + key icons)
- **Instagram Story**: 1080px × 1920px (9:16 crop, full vertical layout)
- **Twitter/X**: 1200px × 675px (16:9 crop, hero number + ROI visual)
- **Facebook**: 1200px × 630px (1.91:1 crop, hero number + icons)
- **Color Mode**: RGB
- **Format**: PNG for graphics with transparency, JPG for full posters

### Animation Version (Digital Screens, Social Media)
- **Hero number counts up**: £1 → £543 (2 seconds)
- **Icons appear sequentially**: One every 0.5 seconds (3.5 seconds total)
- **Bar graph grows**: Bars extend from left to right (2 seconds)
- **Population grid fills**: Icons appear in waves (2 seconds)
- **ROI coins multiply**: 1 coin → 8-12 coins (1.5 seconds)
- **Total runtime**: 11 seconds loop
- **Format**: MP4 or GIF
- **Accessibility**: Include captions for all text

## Icon Design Guidelines

### Style
- **Flat design** (no gradients, shadows, or 3D effects)
- **Bold outlines** (3-4pt stroke weight)
- **Simplified shapes** (recognizable at small sizes)
- **Consistent proportions** (all icons same height ~80pt)
- **Warm Gold fill** with white outline on red background

### Creation
- Vector format (SVG, AI, or similar)
- Scalable without quality loss
- Minimum size: 60px × 60px
- Maximum size: 200px × 200px
- Export as PNG with transparent background for digital use

## Brand Alignment

**BLKOUT Revolutionary Red Theme**:
- Primary red as background = bold, revolutionary, impossible to ignore
- Aligns with brand's liberation-first values

**Community-Centered Visual Language**:
- Icons show infrastructure that serves community
- Grid of people makes 35,000 visible and human
- Warm gold = welcoming, community wealth, not cold data

**Liberation Aesthetics**:
- Rejects corporate minimalism through bold color use
- Embraces cultural richness through varied iconography
- Revolutionary energy in design (red, bold type, infographics)

**Democratic Transparency**:
- Clear information hierarchy
- All costs visible and explained through visuals
- No hidden complexity, accessible to all
